§ 02 A Tuesday Morning

From inbox to counter-offer
in fourteen minutes.

The opposing agent emails their contract at 09:14. By 10:14, you've reviewed it, your client has read the briefing, and the counter-offer is out — without skipping anything you'd normally catch.

Multiply that across thirty deals a year. The math is obvious.

Tuesday · 14 minutes
09:14  Counter-party sends contract

10:02  Counter drafted from FinePrint items
