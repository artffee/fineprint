What happens to my contract? Nothing, after it's read. The file is sent to the analyzer, processed in memory, and the response is returned to you. We don't keep a copy. For full sovereignty, self-host the open-source engine — github.com/fineprintdoc/fineprint.
A helper, not a lawyer FinePrint is a sharper read of your contract. It surfaces what's worth asking about — not a legal opinion. For binding decisions on your deal, please bring this to a licensed real-estate attorney in your state. We make their time more valuable.
